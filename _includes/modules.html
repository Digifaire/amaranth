Modules

Each module is a building blocks of an elegant grant application kit.

Find Sponsors

Explore the world of grants, and sponsorship, and find stakeholders appropriate for your organizational mission and vision.

Review Guidelines

Critically review the criteria set by each spoonsoring organization, to ensure the development of a technically complete application kit.

Research Reviewers

Know the reviewers of the foundation, organization, or sponsor who have been bestowed with the responsibility of advancing the organizational mission by helping develop the finest applicants.

Collaborate

If your organization suffers from deficiencies, remedy the weakness by partnering with industry experts.

Define Problem & Present Solution

Each sponsoring organizaton has a mission to help the world make progress. Define the problem in a way, which advances the cause of the sponsor, and present a relevant solution for the challenge.

Craft Narrative & Assemble Kit

Crafting narrative is like developing the season finale, for delighting the stakeholders with the storyline building upto the climax, and concluding with a satisfying resolution for all parties.
